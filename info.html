Our Society

Feeding Hunger first; wisdom next!! Jeevakarunyaa Foundation aims to help each person reach their fullest potential. An individual may find peace and harmony only within themselves. By doing so, we can bring all of humanity to embrace peace. Since its inception, the foundation has been offering Annadhanam to hundreds of people every day. There is an inherent understanding that without addressing the necessities of an individual, spiritual receptivity remains dormant. Through various programs and initiatives, Our main endeavor is to enhance the primary living conditions of the underprivileged and accelerate the flowering of human consciousness.
